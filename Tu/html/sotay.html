The most detailed guide to applying for a visa
Visa Guide

Visa (also known as visa or entry visa) is a stamp in a passport that shows that an individual is authorized to enter a country. In addition, there are some countries that do not require a visa in some cases, often the result of a compromise between that country and the country of the visa applicant. There are two main types of visas: immigrant visas and non-immigrant visas.
Immigrant Visa: Used to enter and settle in a country under the following categories such as parents sponsoring children, spouses...
Non-immigrant visa: Used to enter a country within a permitted period of time.
